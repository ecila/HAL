Registration

Registration will open in April, 2016.

Registration fees will be kept low (above £80, but below £200): they will pay for lunches, coffee and tea, room rentals and other related costs of running an academic conference. There will be a student discount.

Registration fees include:
- Attendance to paper and poster sessions, concerts, workshops and keynote
- Conference guide and electronic proceedings
- Lunch and coffee on all the days

Accomodation is not included in conference fees, but we recommend booking on-campus rooms with Residential Services, or if you would like to spend the night in the city, the Bed and Breakfasts guide could be helpful in finding good prices. Brighton is very popular destination with tourists in the summer, so booking early is a good idea.
